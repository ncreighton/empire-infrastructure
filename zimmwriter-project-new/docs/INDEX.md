### FAQ Options
- `No FAQ`
- `FAQ + Long Answers`
- `FAQ + Short Answers`

### Voice Options
- `Second Person (You, Your, Yours)`
- `First Person Singular (I, Me, My)`
- `First Person Plural (We, Us, Our)`
- `Third Person (He, She, They)`
- `Professional (minimal pronouns)`
- `None`

### Section Length
- `Tiny`
- `Short`
- `Medium`
- `Long`

### AI Outline Quality
- `Normal $`
- `High $`

### H2 Count
- `Automatic`
- Fixed numbers (1-15+)
