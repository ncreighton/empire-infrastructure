### Post 4: Monthly Full Moon Ritual — March Snow Moon

**Tier:** Candlelight Circle+ (all members)
**Category:** Moon Ritual

```
Title: 🌕 March Full Moon Ritual: Snow Moon Cleansing & Renewal

The March Full Moon (Snow Moon / Worm Moon) rises on March 14, 2026 at 2:55 AM ET in Virgo.

This is a powerful moon for cleansing, purification, and preparing for the spring ahead. Virgo's energy adds precision and healing focus. Perfect timing as we approach Ostara.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🕐 OPTIMAL TIMING
Peak energy: March 14, 2-4 AM (or evening of March 13)
Workable window: March 13-15
Best planetary hour: Moon hour (use a planetary hour calculator for your timezone)

🛒 WHAT YOU'LL NEED
— 1 white candle (purification)
— 1 green candle (renewal, spring energy)
— Salt water in a small bowl
— Rosemary sprig or dried rosemary
— A piece of paper and pen
— Optional: clear quartz, selenite, or amethyst
— Optional: lavender or rosemary incense

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

THE RITUAL

1. PREPARE YOUR SPACE (5 min)
   Cleanse your area with incense smoke or by sprinkling salt water. Place your candles, crystals, and rosemary on your altar or workspace. Take three deep, grounding breaths.

2. CAST YOUR CIRCLE (optional, 3 min)
   Walk clockwise around your space, pointing your finger or wand, and say:
   "I cast this circle as sacred space, between the worlds, outside of time. Only love and light may enter here."

3. LIGHT & INVOKE (3 min)
   Light the white candle and say:
   "By the light of the Snow Moon, I call in purification. Let what no longer serves me be washed clean."

   Light the green candle and say:
   "By the green flame of renewal, I call in spring's promise. Let new growth take root where old patterns once lived."

4. RELEASE WORKING (10 min)
   On your paper, write down 3-5 things you want to release with the melting snow — habits, fears, thought patterns, relationships, or energies that have run their course.

   Read each one aloud, then say after each:
   "I release you with gratitude for what you taught me. You are no longer needed. I am free."

   Dip the rosemary sprig in salt water and flick droplets over the paper to cleanse it.

   Safely burn the paper in a fireproof dish (or tear it into small pieces if fire isn't available).

5. RENEWAL INTENTION (10 min)
   On a fresh piece of paper, write 3 intentions for the spring ahead. Be specific — Virgo rewards precision.

   Hold the paper between your palms and say:
   "As the Snow Moon illuminates the path ahead, I plant these seeds of intention. May they grow strong as the wheel turns toward Ostara. So mote it be."

   Place this paper under the green candle or on your altar where you'll see it daily.

6. CLOSE (5 min)
   Thank the moon for her light. Snuff your candles (don't blow).
   If you cast a circle, walk counterclockwise and say:
   "The circle is open but never broken. Blessed be."

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📝 REFLECTION PROMPTS
After the ritual, journal on these:
— What did it feel like to name what you're releasing?
— Which renewal intention feels most alive to you?
— What one action can you take this week to support your strongest intention?

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Next Full Moon ritual: April 13 (Pink Moon in Libra) — focused on balance and relationship magick. 🌕

Blessed be your Snow Moon working. 🌙
```

**Tier:** Moonlit Coven+ (Moonlit + High Priestess)

```
Title: 🌸 March Spell Kit: Ostara Egg Blessing & Spring Intention Planting

Your first monthly spell kit! Ostara (Spring Equinox, March 20) is the perfect time for this working — a two-part kit combining an egg blessing for new beginnings with a literal intention planting ritual.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PART 1: OSTARA EGG BLESSING

The egg is Ostara's most powerful symbol — potential, new life, the promise of what's to come.

Materials needed:
— 1 hard-boiled egg (or wooden/ceramic egg if you prefer)
— Gold or yellow paint/marker (solar energy)
— Green paint/marker (growth)
— A small dish of soil
— Spring flowers (fresh or dried — daffodils, tulips, crocus, or any spring bloom)

Instructions:
1. On the night before Ostara, hold the egg and meditate on one thing you want to "hatch" this spring.
2. Using gold, draw a sun symbol on one side of the egg. This represents the returning light.
3. Using green, draw a spiral on the other side. This represents growth and unfolding.
4. Hold the egg between your palms and speak your intention aloud three times.
5. Place the egg in the dish of soil, surrounded by spring flowers.
6. Leave on your altar through the equinox (March 20-21).
7. After the equinox, bury the egg in your garden or a potted plant. Your intention is planted.

(If using a real egg and can't bury it, return it to the earth in a compost or outdoor space.)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PART 2: INTENTION SEED PLANTING RITUAL

This is literal magick — combining spell craft with the act of growing something.

Materials needed:
— Small pot with soil (or outdoor garden space)
— Seeds that correspond to your intention:
  • Basil = prosperity, protection
  • Lavender = peace, love, healing
  • Rosemary = memory, protection, purification
  • Mint = abundance, energy, communication
  • Chamomile = calm, patience, sun energy
  • Marigold = success, legal matters, solar power
— Water
— A green candle
— Paper and pen

Instructions:
1. On March 20 (Ostara), light the green candle.
2. Write your spring intentions on the paper — one per line, no more than three.
3. Read them aloud, then fold the paper toward you three times (drawing energy in).
4. Place the folded paper at the bottom of the pot, cover with soil.
5. Plant your chosen seeds on top.
6. Water them while saying: "As these seeds grow, so grows my intention. As spring returns the light, so returns my [intention]. With the turning of the Wheel, so mote it be."
7. Snuff the candle. Tend your seeds daily — tending the plant IS tending the spell.

As the plant grows, your intention grows. If the plant thrives, it's a sign of strong manifestation energy. If it struggles, it's an invitation to examine what's blocking your intention.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📝 JOURNAL PROMPTS
— What am I ready to "hatch" this spring?
— What needed the winter's rest, and is now ready to wake?
— Where do I need more light in my life?
```

```
Peak energy: April 13, 2026
Workable window: April 12-14
Best for: Love, balance, justice, partnership workings

🛒 WHAT YOU'LL NEED
— 1 pink candle (love, gentleness)
— 1 white candle (balance, purity)
— Rose quartz (or any love-associated crystal)
— Rose petals (fresh or dried) or rose water
— 2 small pieces of paper and a pen
— A small bowl of water
— Optional: rose or jasmine incense

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

THE RITUAL

1. PREPARE (5 min)
   Cleanse your space. Arrange the two candles side by side — pink on the left (receiving hand), white on the right (giving hand). Place the rose quartz between them. Scatter rose petals around your workspace.

2. GROUND & CENTER (3 min)
   Close your eyes. Take 5 deep breaths. With each exhale, release tension. With each inhale, draw in the moon's silvery light. Feel yourself balanced between earth and sky.

3. THE BALANCE WORKING (15 min)
   On the first paper, write: "Where I give too much" — list areas where you over-extend, over-give, or lose yourself.
   On the second paper, write: "Where I hold back" — list areas where you don't receive, don't ask for help, or don't let love in.

   Light the white candle and read the "give too much" list. Say:
   "I honor my generosity, and I reclaim my boundaries. Giving and receiving are one breath."

   Light the pink candle and read the "hold back" list. Say:
   "I honor my protection, and I open my heart. Receiving is not weakness — it is trust."

   Place both papers in the bowl of water, overlapping them. Watch the ink begin to blur and merge. Say:
   "Under the Pink Moon's light, I find my balance. I give freely. I receive gracefully. I am whole."

4. SELF-LOVE SEAL (5 min)
   Hold the rose quartz to your heart. Name three things you love about yourself — say them out loud. This is the hardest part of the ritual and the most important.

5. CLOSE (3 min)
   Thank the Pink Moon. Snuff both candles simultaneously if possible (symbolic of balance). Pour the water with the papers onto the earth (garden, potted plant, or outside).

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📝 REFLECTION PROMPTS
— Where in my life is the give-receive balance off?
— What would it feel like to receive as freely as I give?
— Who in my life models healthy balance?
```
