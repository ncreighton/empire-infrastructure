## Production Notes

- **Write moon rituals 5-7 days ahead** — members need time to gather materials
- **Spell kits go out 1st of the month** — gives 4 weeks to use
- **Q&A threads close after 2 weeks** — ensures timely answers
- **Library drops are one-time** (March only) — new HP members get retroactive access
- **Tarot pulls are pre-written** but posted as if live — keeps consistent schedule even during busy weeks
- **Quarterly workbooks need 2-3 weeks of lead time** — they're the most production-heavy content
