## Pricing Strategy Notes

| Tier | Monthly | Annual (if offered) | BMC Fee (5%) | You Receive |
|------|---------|---------------------|--------------|-------------|
| Candlelight | $3 | $30 | $0.15 | $2.85 |
| Moonlit Coven | $7 | $70 | $0.35 | $6.65 |
| High Priestess | $15 | $150 | $0.75 | $14.25 |

**Why these prices:**
- $3 is an impulse-friendly entry point (same as a "potion" tip)
- $7 hits the sweet spot for dedicated hobbyists
- $15 is justified by the $390+ library access alone (96% discount vs buying individually)
- All prices set at or above Etsy equivalents to prevent cannibalization
